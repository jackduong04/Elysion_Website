Soft Washing

Low-Pressure Cleaning for Homes, Buildings, and Delicate Surfaces

Not every surface can handle the brute force of pressure washing - and that's where soft washing comes in. Elysion offers professional soft washing services across Auckland, using low-pressure application and eco-friendly cleaning agents to remove dirt, mold, mildew, and grime without damaging your paintwork, cladding, or landscaping.

It's the safe, effective way to clean and protect your property - especially weatherboard, stucco, painted surfaces, and composite cladding.

Why Choose Soft Washing Instead of Pressure Washing?

Safe for delicate surfaces - no cracked paint, blown seals, or damage to fixtures.

Deep-acting detergents that targets organic growth like moss, algae, lichen, and mould.

Longer-lasting clean - residual treatment slows regrowth.

Eco-friendly options - biodegradable and pet-safe formulas available.

Recommended for pre-paint cleans - cleans surfaces without stripping them .

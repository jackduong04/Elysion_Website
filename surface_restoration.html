Surface Restoration

Restore Your Exterior Surfaces Before the Problems Get Worse

Peeling paint, soft timber, rotting fascia boards - these are more than cosmetic issues. Left unchecked, they lead to bigger problems and costlier fixes. That's why Elysion offers professional surface restoration services throughout Auckland, repairing and restoring exteriors before we repaint or recoat.

Whether you're refreshing a property, selling a home, or just tidying up damage over time, we'll make sure your surfaces are solid, safe, and ready for a fresh new finish.

Timber Repairs

Rotten weatherboards, window trims, and fascia board replacement.

Rotted post or rail removal.

Minor structural or framing fixes where required.

Treated timber replacements to match existing structure.

Surface Prep & Recoating Readiness

Paint stripping and sanding.

Water damage assessment and repair.

Crack filling, puttying, and sealing.

Surface priming for timber, masonry, or concrete.

Mould & Mildew Treatment

Anti-fungal treatments for recurring mould.

Safe removal of black staining and lichen.

Prepping surfaces before repainting or oiling.

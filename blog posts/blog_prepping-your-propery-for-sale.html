The Ultimate Guide to Prepping Your Property for Sale

Posted on May 29, 2025

Make your home stand out, sell faster, and get top dollar

Selling your home isn't just about listing it on the market - it's about creating a first impression that sticks. From the moment potential buyers pull up outside, your property needs to look loved, cared for, and move-in ready. Here's how to do it properly, without wasting time or money.

1. Start With a Full Outdoor Clear-Out

Overgrown hedges, dying plants, and weeds can quickly make your home look neglected. Start by removing:

  • Dead plants and debris
  • Weeds from garden beds and paths
  • Excess leaves, branches, and rubbish

A tidy garden instantly gives the impression that the property has been well looked after.

2. Refresh the Lawns and Edges

Healthy, green grass makes a huge difference to your curb appeal. If your lawn is patchy or overgrown:

  • Mow and edge everything cleanly
  • Fertilise or reseed as needed
  • Consider rolling or topdressing for a smooth finish

3. Pressure Wash Hard Surfaces

Paths, driveways, decks, and even house exteriors accumulate years of grime, moss, and algae. A professional pressure wash can brighten your property instantly—making concrete look new and wood look fresh.

  • Wash all concrete paths, patios, decks, driveways
  • Clean the house exterior (consider softwashing for painted areas)
  • Wash fences and retaining walls for a complete refresh

4. Trim and Shape Hedges and Trees

Buyers notice the details, and wild hedges or low-hanging branches can make outdoor areas feel cluttered. Professional shaping gives a manicured, well-maintained look.

  • Trim hedges level and shape them for neatness
  • Raise tree canopies to open up space
  • Cut back anything encroaching on paths or windows

5. Tidy Up the Entrance

The front entrance is where your buyer will pause and reflect—make sure it wows them.

  • Wash down the front door and entranceway
  • Add a clean doormat and potted plants
  • Make sure all lighting works and shines bright at night

6. Final Touches That Make a Difference

If you want to go the extra mile:

  • Bark mulch garden beds to suppress weeds and sharpen the look
  • Fix or repaint fences and gates
  • Add a few seasonal flowers for pops of colour

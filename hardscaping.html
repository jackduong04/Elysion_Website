Hardscaping

Give Your Outdoor Space Structure, Style, and Purpose

Hardscaping is the foundation of great landscaping - literally. It's what defines your space, guides foot traffic, creates levels, and frames everything else you plant. At Elysion, we design and construct hardscape elements that are as durable as they are beautiful.

From modern decks and patios to timeless retaining walls and pathways, our Auckland hardscaping team blends form with function for results that elevate your entire outdoor environment.

Decking

Timber or composite decks, custom built to match your home's flow and layout.

Fencing & Gates

Stylish, secure boundaries that enhance privacy and curb appeal.

Retaining Walls

Timber, block, or concrete solutions that hold back land and level up your space.

Paving & Paths

From clean concrete to decorative stonework - we create walkways with impact.

Garden Edging & Borders

Keep things neat with crisp lawn edges, sleepers, or paver borders.

Steps, Terraces & Raised Beds

Add dimension and accessibility to your landscape design.
